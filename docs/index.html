SpeechCraft

Craft your spoken words into text—anywhere on your Mac.

Download on GitHub

Key Features

  • Quick Transcription: Press Option+S to record your voice and automatically insert text at the cursor.
  • Smart Editing: Press Option+Shift+S to speak an instruction on selected text (e.g., “fix grammar”) and let GPT‑4 apply it instantly.
  • Menu‑Bar Status: The colored circle shows green (ready), red (recording), or blue (processing).

Getting Started

  1. Download the latest SpeechCraft.app from GitHub Releases.
  2. Move the app into your /Applications folder.
  3. Open SpeechCraft; grant Accessibility and Microphone permissions when prompted.
  4. Use Option+S or Option+Shift+S anywhere you type.
